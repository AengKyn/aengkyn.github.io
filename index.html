About

Who
I am

Creative, detail-focused, and brand-driven — I bring over 13 years of experience in print and digital design, specialising in the swimwear and apparel industry.

I thrive in collaborative environments where creativity and clarity come first. With a no-fluff, results-oriented approach, I'm passionate about delivering bold, effective design that communicates clearly and performs.

I have a proven track record of building and leading high-performing creative teams, nurturing both brand integrity and individual growth. My experience spans local and global campaigns across print and digital platforms — from large-scale corporate projects to bespoke solutions for small businesses.
